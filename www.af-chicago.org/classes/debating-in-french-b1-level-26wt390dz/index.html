        Debating in French (B1 Level)

        Class information

        Costs

        Tuition

        No one loves arguing more than the French! The debate tradition whether in familly discusssions, politics or acaedmics is deeply roooted in France's ethos and how they perceive the language's role and purpose. This course will teach you how to use your French to feel more at ease in this execrcise. It is also a great preparation course for proficiency exams like DELF/DALF or TCF/TEF.
